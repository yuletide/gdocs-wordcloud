Summary

  1. PHA residents qualify for free netbooks after completing 8-hour computer skills training
  2. We participated on pickup day by helping each recipient unbox and set up netbooks, also prepped some questions in advance to learn about digital divide. 115 netbooks distributed (we were 2 of 4-5 people who helped set up 115 in total)
  4. Most recipients had a relative or friend who could act as online coach, but some (10%) did not.
